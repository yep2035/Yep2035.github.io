Our Campaign Journey

December 2024: Feeding the Hungry Campaign

Kickstart the initiative with a food drive to provide meals for the homeless and less privileged during the festive season.

January 2025: Clothing Drive for the Homeless

Collect and distribute clothing, blankets, and essentials to those in need, focusing on the colder months.

February 2025: Free Medical Outreach

Partner with healthcare professionals to provide free checkups, medications, and consultations in underserved communities.

March 2025: Skills Acquisition Workshop

Host workshops on graphic design, coding, baking, and tailoring to empower youths with practical, income-generating skills

April 2025: Educational Support Drive

Distribute learning materials, such as books and stationery, and launch free tutorial programs for struggling students.
